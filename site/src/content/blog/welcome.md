Hi, and thanks for stopping by.

This is the first post on what we hope becomes a quiet, steady record of
our family's adoption journey. We're not sure yet how often we'll write
here. Probably whenever something feels worth holding onto — a milestone,
a hard day, a small joy.

If you're reading this and you're an expectant parent considering
adoption, please know: there's no rush, no pressure, and no expectation.
Take whatever time you need. We'll be here.

— Daniel & Katie
