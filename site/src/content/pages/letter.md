Dear Friend,

Thank you for taking the time to read this. We know you are making one
of the most important decisions of your life, and we want you to know
that we see you, we respect you, and we honor whatever path you choose.

We're Daniel and Katie. We live in Holladay, Utah with our two kids, E
and C, and we're hoping to grow our family through open adoption.

We believe in open adoption, not as an obligation but as a gift to
your child and to you. We want you to be part of this child's story,
always. That means photos, updates, calls, and visits, as much or as
little as feels right for you.

Whatever you decide, we want you to know: you are brave, you are loved,
and your child is lucky to have a parent who cares this much about their
future.

With love and hope,

Daniel, Katie, E & C Sellers
